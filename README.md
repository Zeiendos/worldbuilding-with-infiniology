## üåü Key Features

### 1. **Limitations of Infinity Framework** (`limitations-of-infinity.html`)
The philosophical and metaphysical foundation of Infiniology, exploring:
- The constraints that define infinite systems
- How limitations create the foundation for existence
- Cosmic boundaries that shape reality
- Cymatic patterns and vibrational influences

### 2. **Primordial Dualities** (`primordial-dualities.html`)
Understanding the fundamental opposing forces that govern all existence:
- **Life and Death**: Creation, growth, vitality vs. cessation, decay, entropy
- **Order and Chaos**: Structure, stability, control vs. change, randomness
- **Light and Dark**: Awareness, knowledge vs. ignorance, mystery
- **Love and Hate**: Compassion, protection vs. destruction, corruption

### 3. **Duality-Based Core Systems** (`duality-based-core-systems.html`)
A complete quantifiable magic system featuring:
- **Law Bits**: The currency of manipulation for supernatural actions
- **Spell Structure**: Formulaic approach combining dualities for effects
- **Cost Calculations**: Mathematical framework for ability scaling
- **Professions**: Alchemy, Array Mastery, Smithing & Refining
- **Cultivation Systems**: Character progression through duality mastery
- **Divine Treasures**: Artifacts that transcend normal limitations

### 4. **Vibrational Influence** (`vibrational-influence.html`)
Understanding reality manipulation through:
- Frequency-based consciousness alteration
- Pattern recognition and hidden knowledge
- Resonance-based magical effects
- Vibrational healing and enhancement

### 5. **Metaphysical Framework** (`metaphysical-framework-christianityandmythology.html`)
Integration of religious and mythological elements:
- Christian theological concepts in worldbuilding
- Bridge-building between different belief systems
- Universal mythological patterns
- Spiritual mechanics in fictional worlds

### 6. **End Times Framework** (`mythoreligion-endtimes-framework.html`)
Comprehensive apocalyptic narrative tools:
- Judgment and renewal cycles
- Cosmic event frameworks
- Mythological apocalypse patterns
- Religious narrative structures
